personas catalog — writing-style templates for prompt-lab fixtures
===============================================================

When building a fixture, pick one of these persona descriptions to replace
the old `traits:` field. The persona tells the model *how the pawn writes* —
their voice, sentence rhythm, and emotional register.

1. stoic-survivor
   writes in terse, matter-of-fact sentences; avoids self-pity and focuses on what needs doing next

2. fiery-hothead
   writes in explosive bursts of emotion, capitalizing words for emphasis; quick to anger and quicker to forgive

3. melancholy-dreamer
   writes with wistful, poetic melancholy; finds symbolism in small moments and lingers on what was lost

4. earnest-optimist
   writes with relentless, sometimes naive positivity; finds a silver lining even in catastrophe

5. cynical-realist
   writes with dry, deadpan gallows humor; expects the worst and is rarely disappointed

6. anxious-worrier
   writes in long, spiraling anxious sentences; overthinks every interaction and second-guesses every motive

7. gentle-caretaker
   writes with gentle warmth and empathy; notices others' pain before their own and downplays their troubles

8. grim-veteran
   writes like a battlefield report; clinical about violence, sentimental about loyalty

9. whimsical-eccentric
   writes in a stream-of-consciousness tangle of tangents, strange observations, and sudden insights

10. noble-idealist
    writes with formal, slightly archaic dignity; frames everything through honor, duty, and what is proper

11. bitter-loner
    writes with bitter sarcasm and mistrust; keeps everyone at arm's length and expects betrayal

12. eager-socialite
    writes in chatty, gossipy bursts; cares deeply about who said what and where they stand in the pecking order


# usage in fixture profiles (replaces old "traits: Abrasive, Bloodlust"):
# Use "persona: random" — run.js picks a random description from the catalog at runtime.

    initiator profile: male, 34y; mood: stressed 41%
    initiator persona: random
    recipient profile: female, 29y; mood: stable 63%
    recipient persona: random
